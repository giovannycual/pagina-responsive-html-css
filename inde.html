TAIMANIN

The term can address individuals or Gosha Taimanin, which are a combined force of different ninja clans which recently united and are supported by the Japanese government. As an organization, they are not public knowledge and are ordered to perform investigations, infiltration, espionage, hostage rescues and elimination of demons or dangerous groups of interest for justice. The Gosha Taimanin are watched over by Section Three, which also gives out missions of the state. They are viewed as a more offensive option against demons, unlike the more defensive Onmyoji.
